About Us

We're a young non-profit organization aimed at helping give tools to young students to embrace their passion for Engineering and STEM in general. Our goal is to promote STEM to students all over the Orange County Community and eventually spread across California and then the US. Our main program is the Team America Rocketry Challenge. We field three teams to compete against the hundreds of others across the United States, giving students first hand experience on designing and building products for the Aerospace and Physics Field.
